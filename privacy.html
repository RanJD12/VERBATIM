Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

VERBATIM is a Progressive Web App. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how we operate.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require a login, and we do not have access to your device's contacts, location, or files.

2. Third-Party Services

This app is hosted on GitHub Pages. You can view their privacy practices on the GitHub Privacy Statement page. We also use libraries like Three.js and Tone.js which run locally in your browser and do not collect data.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This app uses a Service Worker to cache files for offline use. This is strictly for performance and does not track your activity.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
